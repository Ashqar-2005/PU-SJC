About the Program

A Pioneering Collaboration

The MBA Twinning Program is a unique partnership between St. Joseph's College (Autonomous) and Pondicherry University, designed to deliver a globally competitive business education with dual certification.

Leverage the academic excellence of St. Joseph's NAAC A++ accreditation and Pondicherry University's UGC recognition.
